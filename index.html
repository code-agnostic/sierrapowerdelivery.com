The Location

The Corridor.
Captured.

Carson City sits at the intersection of three of the West's most-traveled EV routes — Reno, Lake Tahoe, and the California border. Every driver on this corridor passes through. We built here intentionally.

Reno
35 min south on US-395. Ideal first or last charge on a Sierra crossing.
Lake Tahoe
30 min west via US-50. The only V4 array between Tahoe and Reno.
California
45 min to the state line. A natural range buffer for interstate travelers.
Opening 2027

The Experience

Pull In.
Plug In.
Go.

Pull up, plug in, and charge at up to 500kW. Works with Tesla's Magic Dock for CCS vehicles — no adapter needed.

01
Arrive
Pull up to any open stall. All 8 are forward-facing with extra-long V4 cables that reach any vehicle, any side.
02
Connect
Plug in. Tesla vehicles connect natively. All other EVs use the integrated Magic Dock CCS connector — no adapter required.
03
Charge
Up to 500kW delivered. A 15–20 minute session adds 150–200 miles for most vehicles at peak state of charge.

About

Built by People
Who've Done This.

Sierra Power Delivery LLC was founded by a team with deep roots in the EV industry. We've been driving electric since 2015 — long before charging infrastructure was taken for granted — and we've spent years on the inside helping build it.

Our team includes former Tesla experience and hands-on work in the DC fast charging industry, including leading the launch of a 40-stall flagship charging site. We know what it takes to plan, permit, build, and operate high-performance charging at scale.

Sierra Power Delivery is our answer to a gap we've seen firsthand: the Reno–Tahoe–Carson corridor deserves infrastructure that matches the terrain — powerful, reliable, and built for the long haul.
